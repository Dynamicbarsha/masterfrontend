I am item b

Description

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

6 cups frozen mixed berries ½ cup white sugar ¼ cup orange juice 1 ½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest 2 tablespoons cornstarch 2 tablespoons water

Steps

Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker. Cover and cook on High until bubbling, about 1 1/2 hours. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Stir into berry mixture. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
